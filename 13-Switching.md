# Switching

## What is Switching?

Switching is the process of forwarding data within the same network.

---

## Switch

A switch operates at Layer 2 of the OSI Model.

Functions:

- Learn MAC Addresses
- Forward Frames
- Reduce Collisions

---

## MAC Address Table

Switches maintain a MAC Table.

Example:

| MAC Address | Port |
|-------------|------|
| 00:11:22:33 | 1 |
| AA:BB:CC:DD | 2 |

---

## Benefits

- Faster Communication
- Better Efficiency
- Reduced Network Traffic

# Subnetting

Subnetting divides a large network into smaller networks.

---

## Why Subnet?

- Better Security
- Better Performance
- Easier Management

---

## Example

Network:

192.168.1.0/24

Subnet Mask:

255.255.255.0

Usable Hosts:

192.168.1.1

to

192.168.1.254

Broadcast Address:

192.168.1.255

---

## CIDR Notation

Examples:

/24

/16

/8

CIDR represents network size.

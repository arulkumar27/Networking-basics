# VLAN

## What is VLAN?

VLAN stands for Virtual Local Area Network.

It logically separates devices into different networks.

---

## Example

VLAN 10 = HR

VLAN 20 = Finance

VLAN 30 = IT

---

## Benefits

- Better Security
- Traffic Isolation
- Reduced Broadcasts
- Easier Management

---

## Access Port

Belongs to a single VLAN.

Example:

PC connected to Switch.

---

## Trunk Port

Carries multiple VLANs.

Example:

Switch-to-Switch Connection.

# VPN

## What is VPN?

VPN stands for Virtual Private Network.

It creates an encrypted tunnel over the Internet.

---

## Benefits

- Privacy
- Security
- Remote Access

---

## VPN Types

### Remote Access VPN

Used by employees connecting from home.

---

### Site-to-Site VPN

Connects two offices together.

---

## VPN Workflow

User

↓

VPN Tunnel

↓

Company Network

All traffic is encrypted.
